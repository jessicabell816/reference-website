Naming Conventions

  • All lowercase.
  • No spaces.
  • Only letters, numbers and dashes.

Commit Messages

    Minimum 3 words & 10 Characters. Proper grammer and spelling. Starts with capital, ends with no period. Must start with an imperative verb: Create, Fix, Add, Solve, etc.
